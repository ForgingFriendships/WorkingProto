Inspiration





Hannah

Forging Friendships was created to honor and celebrate the life of Hannah Noelle who passed away on October 26, 2013.

Hannah’s nickname was “Sunshine Girl,” which described her perfectly. She was an honor role student and the President of her eighth-grade class. Bi-lingual in both English and Spanish, Hannah enjoyed learning in an immersion school environment.

Hannah loved nature and enjoyed to bake, travel and read... She was an aspiring drummer and an avid mogul skier showing no mercy to the steep, wintery slopes of Lake Tahoe.

Hannah lit up the world with her sweet smile; she is missed beyond measure and will be treasured in our hearts, always.




Zachary

Zachary David is Hannah’s younger cousin who has Cerebral Palsy, severe brain malformation, Hydrocephalus and is wheelchair dependent. Zac has very little language and although he is extremely limited in fine and gross motor skills, he certainly makes up for that with in his infectious laugh, joyful spirit and capacity to genuinely love everyone he meets.

Despite their obvious physical and intellectual differences, Zac and Hannah truly enjoyed each other’s company and were complete equals because of the love and friendship they shared together.

The inspiration behind Forging Friendships is Zac and Hannah’s uncommon kindness towards each other. Cousins? Yes. Friends? Ab-so-lutely!
